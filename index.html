Rods and Cones work differently

Rods help you see in low light. Cones help to distinguish colors and are more active in bright light.

High contrast is hard on the eyes, so in a dark room it is better to use a dark theme or night mode.

Melatonin

Melatonin is a hormone that your brain produces in response to darkness.
It helps with the timing of your circadian rhythms (24-hour internal clock) and with sleep.

Bright light interferes with melatonin production
